## Core Idea

Extend Anderson framework from chemical QS to ALL 8 microbial communication
modes. 6 of 8 modes are subject to Anderson localization. Only contact-dependent
(Myxococcus) and nanowire (Geobacter) are immune.

## Key Findings

- 8 signaling modes catalogued: AHL, AI-2, K+ wave, mechanical, biophoton, nanowire, contact, peptide QS
- 6/8 modes Anderson-susceptible, 2/8 immune
- Signaling portfolio scales with geometry: 3D (all modes) → 2D (contact only) → planktonic (none)
- W_c = 16.26 (from Exp150) defines the transition for ALL wave-based modes
- This explains why plankton don't signal: ALL wave modes fail, not just chemical QS

## Connection to Sub-thesis 04

Mechanical wave analysis directly feeds sentinel design — only Anderson-immune
modes (contact, nanowire) reliably function across all geometries.
