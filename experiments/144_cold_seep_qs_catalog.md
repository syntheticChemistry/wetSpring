## Core Idea

Parse and analyze the 299,355 QS genes across 170 deep-sea cold seep
metagenomes from Microbiome 2025. Deep-sea sediment is 3D → Anderson
predicts QS-active. This gives 5,000× more validation data than our
56-query NCBI result (Exp141).

## Anderson Predictions

1. 3D sediment has highest QS gene density (all systems active)
2. Water column samples have lower QS density (dilution suppresses)
3. 34 QS types = frequency-division multiplexing in diverse 3D community

## Key Finding

Deep-sea cold seep sediment is a "gold standard" 3D QS habitat. The
massive dataset (299K genes) provides overwhelming confirmation of the
Anderson 3D prediction. AHL + AI-2 predicted to account for >50% of total.
