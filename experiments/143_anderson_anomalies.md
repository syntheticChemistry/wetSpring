## The Three Genuine NP Solutions

1. **V. cholerae — Logic Inversion**: detects signal ABSENCE (low density →
   be virulent). Detecting zero is trivial; no Anderson barrier applies.
   Analogous to reformulating a hard optimization as its dual problem.

2. **M. xanthus — Self-Organized Geometry**: starts 2D, uses
   contact-dependent C-signal (not diffusible, bypasses Anderson) to
   nucleate aggregation, creating the 3D structure that enables diffusible
   A-signal. Bootstrapping: builds the hardware for the algorithm.

3. **Dictyostelium — Signal Relay**: each cell amplifies and retransmits cAMP.
   Active relay defeats localization because each cell is a signal SOURCE,
   not a passive scatterer. The biological repeater network.

## Connection to P≠NP Enzyme Thesis

Anderson localization is the CONSTRAINT. QS in unfavorable geometry is the
NP-HARD PROBLEM. The solutions require HISTORICAL CONTINGENCY — V. cholerae's
inverted logic required prior evolution of standard QS before the inversion
could be beneficial. Like Lenski's Ara-3 citrate: potentiating → actualizing.
