## Core Idea

Test the eavesdropper prediction against published functional metagenomics data.
Activated sludge (3D dense flocs) reported 13 luxI + 30 luxR, giving R:P = 2.3:1.
Anderson predicts this habitat is strongly QS-active with eavesdropper enrichment.

## Key Findings

- R:P = 2.3:1 matches rhizosphere ratio (also 2.3:1) — consistent eavesdropper enrichment in diverse 3D habitats
- Biofilm R:P = 0.8:1 (cooperative, balanced) vs multi-species R:P > 1 (eavesdroppers)
- Hot spring R:P = 0.4:1 (minimal QS investment) — lowest in dataset
- Correlated disorder (Exp151) makes sludge QS even more robust than i.i.d. prediction
- 43 QS genes confirm Anderson extended prediction for 3D dense habitats

Direct validation of the eavesdropper gradient hypothesis across habitat types.
