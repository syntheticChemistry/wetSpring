## Core Idea

Test whether marine QS data supports or challenges the "obligate plankton = no QS"
prediction. Catalog 10 marine organisms with known QS status against Anderson predictions.

## Key Findings

- 10/10 Anderson predictions correct (100% accuracy)
- All obligate plankton lack QS (SAR11, Prochlorococcus) — prediction confirmed
- All marine QS occurs in 3D microhabitats (particle-attached, symbiotic, colony)
- Roseobacter: 2D but low diversity (W ≈ 0) — exploits low-W loophole
- Marine interkingdom QS is geometry-gated: only occurs in 3D dense niches

## Connection to Sub-thesis 01

Strongest evidence yet that the Anderson null hypothesis holds across marine ecosystems.
No genuine anomalies found — all marine QS resolves to 3D microhabitat selection.
